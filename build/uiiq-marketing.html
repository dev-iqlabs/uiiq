What UBMS covers

💌 Email marketing

Campaigns, automated sequences, A/B subject-line testing, contacts, segments, and double opt-in — all delivered via SendGrid. Know your open rates, click rates, and which campaigns drove bookings.

📱 Social media

Compose, schedule, and queue posts across LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X, and TikTok. Platform-specific previews, hashtag groups, bulk CSV scheduling, and a unified planning calendar.

🔍 SEO & analytics

Site crawl, Google Search Console, GA4, PageSpeed, and Core Web Vitals — all in one dashboard. Every issue ranked by impact, with a “Fix with AI” button that drafts the copy change for you.

📈 Paid ads dashboard

Google Ads, Google Merchant Center, and Meta — all visible in one view. See spend, clicks, and channel breakdown without logging into three separate ad platforms.

AI website editor

Connect your WordPress site to UBMS. Chat with AI to propose changes to any page — headlines, copy, meta descriptions. Review it, approve it, publish it. No touching the WordPress editor. No developer needed. Powered by Claude via IQEX.

CRM & contacts

Every customer in one place. Contacts sync automatically from POSM bookings, WooCommerce orders, and email signups. Segment by behaviour, tag by source, and see the full history — every booking, every email, every purchase — on one contact record.

Revenue reporting

Cross-integration revenue report — POSM bookings, WooCommerce orders, till sales, and Xero P&L — all in one view. Period selector, monthly breakdown, CSV and PDF export.
