Built by the team behind Ultimate Image.

UIIQ is developed by IQ Labs, the software division of Ultimate Image Ltd — a UK creative and technology company.

We built UIIQ because we were the customer first. Running events, managing bookings, sending campaigns, and scheduling staff across multiple tools — and paying for the privilege. So we built the platform we wished existed.

POSM started as a booking engine for our own experience businesses. UBMS grew from the marketing back-office we built for our own campaigns. UVOS was born from the operations and HR headaches every growing team eventually hits.

Today, UIIQ powers Ultimate Image and our client tenants. We’re opening it to UK SMEs — venues, experience businesses, and service operators — who deserve enterprise-grade tools without enterprise-grade complexity or price.
