Frequently asked questions

Is there a setup fee?

No. Sign up, configure your account, go live.

What happens at the end of my trial?

You choose a plan and enter your payment details. Nothing is charged during the 14-day trial.

Can I use just one module?

Yes — POSM, UBMS, and UVOS can each be subscribed to independently. You only pay for what you use.

What is the POSM commission on?

Commission applies to bookings processed through the POSM booking engine via Stripe Connect. It does not apply to POSM Till sales. Compare this to Booking.com (15–20%) or Eventbrite (6.5%+) — UIIQ is significantly cheaper at volume.

Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-month?

Yes — upgrades apply immediately, pro-rata. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date.

Is there a minimum contract?

No minimum term. Monthly rolling. Cancel any time from your account settings.

Where is my data stored?

UK-based PostgreSQL database (Neon, eu-west-2 London region). Fully GDPR compliant. Your data never leaves the UK.

Do you offer enterprise or multi-site pricing?

Yes — contact us for multi-venue and franchise pricing. Centralised dashboard, volume discount, custom onboarding.
