How it works

Business cards

1. Order your card

Choose your finish — matte white, gloss, or metal. Upload your design or use our template.

2. We link your card

Tell us your URL or contact page. We register the NFC and QR to your account.

3. Tap or scan — done

Hand it over. They tap or scan. They land exactly where you want them. Every time.

Greeting cards

1. Order your card

Pick a design. We print and post it to you, QR inside.

2. Upload your message

Log in and upload your video, photos, or write a personal message — before or after posting the card.

3. They scan — they see it

The QR inside opens your personal page. No app needed. No account for them. Just your message.
