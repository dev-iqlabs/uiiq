From setup to sold out in three steps

1. Set up your experience

Create sessions, set capacity, configure pricing tiers, add-ons, and custom intake fields. Takes 10 minutes.

2. Take bookings

Online via the embedded booking widget on your site, or walk-ins straight through the POSM Till. Both update the same live capacity.

3. Day of the event

QR check-in on any device, real-time capacity view, till open. Post-event: bookings, revenue, and attendee data flow into your UBMS Marketing dashboard automatically.

AI demand prediction & dynamic pricing

POSM forecasts demand 90 days out per experience and suggests optimal pricing based on inventory, days-to-event, and historical patterns. Right-size your capacity. Stop leaving empty seats. No other SME booking platform has this.

OTA channel management
