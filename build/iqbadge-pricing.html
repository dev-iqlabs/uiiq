Frequently asked questions

Do attendees need an app?

No — any smartphone camera can scan the QR code on the badge. iOS and Android both work. No app, no account, no friction for the person scanning.

How far in advance do we need to order?

Allow 2 weeks minimum — one week for profile collection from attendees, one week for badge production and delivery. Express turnaround available on request.

Can we use our own badge design?

Yes — supply your artwork and we apply the QR to it. We can also design the badge for you as part of the setup fee.

What happens to attendee data after the event?

All profile data is deleted 30 days after event close unless the organiser requests retention for CRM export. We are fully GDPR compliant and attendees are informed at profile submission.

Can badges be time-limited?

Yes — QR access automatically deactivates on the event end date. For staff ID, badges deactivate when a profile is marked inactive in the admin dashboard.

Can attendees update their own profiles?

Yes — each attendee gets a link to update their bio, photo, and contact details before the event. Changes go live immediately.
