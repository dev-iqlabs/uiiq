Frequently asked questions

Do they need an app?

No. Any iPhone or Android can tap NFC or scan the QR using the built-in camera. No app, no account, no friction.

What if I change jobs or my website changes?

Log into your dashboard and update the destination. The physical card stays the same — you never need to reprint.

Can I see who tapped my card?

Yes — your dashboard shows tap and scan counts per card, by week. You see volume; we don’t track individuals.

How long does delivery take?

3 working days standard. Express available on request.

Can I customise the card design?

Yes — upload your own design or use our template. Include your logo, brand colours, and layout. Full print spec provided on order.

How long does the greeting card message stay live?

Forever. Your message page is hosted for the life of the card with no expiry. No renewal needed for greeting cards.
