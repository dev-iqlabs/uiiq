How it works

1. Brief us

Tell us about your event or programme — attendee count, badge design, content brief, and event date.

2. Attendees complete profiles

We send a short digital form. Each attendee adds their photo, bio, and contact details. Takes 3 minutes.

3. Badges delivered

Printed, QR-linked badges arrive at your registration desk. Ready to go.

4. Scan at the event

Attendees scan any badge — profile opens, contact saves. No app. Any smartphone camera.

5. Post-event report

Full engagement data delivered to the organiser. Who connected with whom. How many scans per badge.
