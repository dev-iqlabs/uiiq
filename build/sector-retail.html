POSM Till

Stripe Terminal and SumUp on one device. WooCommerce catalogue loaded in. Mixed payments (card, cash, gift card) on one transaction. Offline mode — keeps working when the internet drops.

WooCommerce sync

UBMS syncs your WooCommerce product catalogue. Orders from your online shop flow into UBMS and trigger UVOS production task workflows. Manage product content from one place.

Workflow orders

WooCommerce order received → UVOS production task created automatically: Print → QC → Pack → Ship. Your fulfilment pipeline is visible to your whole team in real time.

Purchase-based email

Segment your customer list by what they bought. Send re-order reminders, related product suggestions, and loyalty offers — all triggered by purchase behaviour in UBMS.

Gift cards

Branded PDF gift cards sold online or at the till. Redeemable at checkout or via the POSM Till. Balance tracked in real time. Great for seasonal gifting campaigns.

Revenue reporting

UBMS cross-integration revenue report — till sales, WooCommerce orders, and Xero P&L in one view. Period selector, monthly breakdown, CSV and PDF export.

All three modules — connected end to end

Order received → UBMS contacts updated → UVOS task created → POSM Till ready for collection or dispatch. UBMS sends the confirmation email. No manual steps in between.
